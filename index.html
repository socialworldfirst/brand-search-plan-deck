The problem01
The problem

Few people search our name.

In the UK, about 4,400 people a month type "worldfirst" into Google. Revolut gets 301,000. Even Airwallex gets more than double our number.

Our goal is 2 million brand views on Google this year. Half the year is gone and we are at 17%. This deck is the plan to change that, and to be honest about how fast it can change.

What we learned02
What we learned

Ad money alone cannot buy it.

We did the maths on our own ad results, then had it checked three times. US$10,000 a month of boosting buys roughly 200 extra name searches. That is too small for anyone to see on a chart.

We already ran this experiment without meaning to: the UK got about US$40,000 of boost money in the first half. The UK name-search line did not move.

Nobody in our category has ever bought a brand-search line with boosting. So the plan cannot be "spend more."

Why this happens03
Why this happens

People search a name when they remember it and have a reason.

Four things must happen: a person sees us → remembers us → one day needs us → and types our name instead of a general phrase. Our data shows exactly which links are broken:

  • Seeing us: works. 14 million ad views, 1.5 million video views in half a year.
  • Remembering us: broken. Our videos are the most-watched per video in the whole field, but people remember the lesson and forget the teacher.
  • Typing our name: broken. We say "good rates." So does everyone. When the need comes, people type "how to pay china supplier" and Google hands them to whoever ranks. Usually not us.
Proof it is the message, not the market: in Chinese, 万里汇 already means "the safe, official one" and people search it with zero ad money behind it. Same company. Same product. Different message. Different result.

The plan04
The plan · Four parts

Fix the broken links, not the budget.

  • 1 · Say one clear thing One message only we can say. Something worth remembering and typing.
  • 2 · Say it in one place All effort on one market until the line moves. Not eight markets at once.
  • 3 · Make news people quote A quarterly WorldFirst index from our own data. News must say our name to use it.
  • 4 · Count it right Today we count 2 words and 1 website. We under-count our own brand.
Parts 1 and 2 fix "remember us." Part 3 reaches people we cannot reach. Part 4 makes the scoreboard true. Boosting stays, but as the helper, not the plan.

Part 1 · The message05
Part 1 · The message

Say the one thing only we can say.

"WorldFirst is the official way to pay 1688 and China suppliers. The money arrives the same day. You cannot get scammed."

  • Wise cannot say it. Airwallex cannot say it. Payoneer cannot say it. We are part of the Ant family. It is simply true.
  • The goal: when a seller thinks "I have to pay my China supplier," one name pops into their head. Ours.
  • "Good rates" gives nobody a reason to search. "The official 1688 payment company" does.
This is not a slogan project. It is the same message repeated in every video, every ad, every post, for months, until the market can finish the sentence for us.
Brand Search Plan · Jul 2026
Part 2 · One place first06
Part 2 · One place first

One market. Repeated. Until it moves.

Last month our boost money went to six regions at once. Split that thin, it is invisible everywhere. The same money in one market is loud enough to matter.

Why the UK first

  • It is our biggest name-search base outside China (4,400 a month).
  • The gap to Airwallex and Payoneer there is about 3x. Everywhere else it is 10x to 100x. The UK is the only market where the gap can close.
  • It is the exact line leadership already tracks.
Brand Search Plan · Jul 2026
Part 3 · Make news07
Part 3 · Make news

Publish news that must say our name.

The WorldFirst Trade Index: every quarter, we publish what small sellers are really paying and buying across borders, from our own payment data. No rival can copy it, because no rival has our data.

  • A news story cannot quote the number without saying "WorldFirst." Every quote is free brand exposure with trust attached.
  • Press carries it to millions of people our own channels will never reach.
  • Every big search jump we found in a year of rival data came from news. None came from posts. Airwallex's best week all year was a funding story.
  • We cannot make funding news (Ant owns us). An index is news we can make ourselves, on schedule.

Part 4 · Count it right08
Part 4 · Count it right

Our scoreboard under-counts us.

Today the goal is tracked on two search words and one website. But a lot of our brand searching happens where that tracker cannot see:

  • Chinese searchers click our own China site (worldfirst.com.cn), our own Zhihu page, and partner pages. Those clicks are real, but they count as zero.
  • Google itself counts many more brand words than we do: spelling mistakes, product names, translations.
The fix is free and takes days: add the China site to our Search Console and count the full brand word list. We are likely closer to the goal than the scoreboard says.

Bonus · Copy Pakistan09
Bonus · Copy what already works

Where money flows, searches follow.

Pakistan searches our name more than almost anywhere, with zero ad money. Why? Because real people get paid through us there. The search is a side effect of the money flow.

  • Payoneer built its whole brand this way: it has been the way Upwork freelancers get paid for 15 years. People search what pays them.
  • Our move: deepen marketplace payout ties so more sellers must know our name, and use real local sellers as the faces of the brand, like Payoneer does in Bangladesh.
This lever belongs to partnerships more than marketing, but it is the strongest long-term engine on this list, so it stays on the page.

The test10
The test

Prove it in 3 months, before we bet a year.

  • Where: UK only. One compare market gets nothing, so we can tell signal from luck.
  • How much: US$25,000–30,000 a month for 3 months. About US$90,000 total.
  • What changes: the creative. The new one-clear-message ads versus our current teaching-style content.
  • How we judge: one number only, name searches in the UK. Not views, not likes.
If the line moves, we scale with confidence. If it does not, we stop and saved a year of brand spend. Either answer is worth US$90K.

Honest timeline11
The honest timeline
2years
To a brand-search line / we are proud of

Every brand that grew name searches took years, not quarters. A fair expectation: 1.5x to 2x in the UK over 18–24 months, with the first real signal readable after the 3-month test. Anyone promising 4x in a year is selling something.

Keep the 2M goal. Move the deadline, fix the scoreboard, and judge this year on whether the line finally starts moving, not on whether it finishes.

The ask12
The ask · Three decisions

Three yes/no decisions.

  • 1 · Approve the UK test US$90K over 3 months, new message versus old content, judged on name searches alone.
  • 2 · Green-light the index One quarter to scope: what data we can publish, who builds it, which press partner launches it.
  • 3 · Fix the scoreboard now Add the China site, count the full brand word list. Free, done in days, no approval cost.
The message gives people something to remember. One market makes it loud enough to stick. The index makes strangers say our name. That is how a search line moves.
